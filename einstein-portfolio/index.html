Albert Einstein

  • German-born theoretical physicist
  • best known for developing the theory of relativity
  • made important contributions to the development of the theory of quantum mechanics
  • received the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics
  • Einsteinium, one of the synthetic elements in the periodic table, was named in his honor.
